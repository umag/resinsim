# Source: Impact of Interface Flexibility on Separation Force in LCD VPP

**"Impact of Interface Flexibility on Separation Force in LCD Vat
Photopolymerization: A Comprehensive Analysis," *ACS Applied Polymer Materials*
2025, doi:10.1021/acsapm.5c00167.** (Paywalled; key numbers via abstract +
ResearchGate preprint.)

## What it is

The rigid-vs-flexible interface study — the source of our 100× and Teflon-10×
figures (which are two *different* comparisons, easily conflated).

## Key data

- **Rigid vs flexible interface: up to ~100× higher separation force** under
  identical conditions.
- **Teflon/FEP is ~10× *higher* than soft PDMS** (stiffer film peels worse — NOT
  a 10× reduction).
- An analytical model based on Kendall adhesion theory predicts peak force within
  **±10–15%**.
