# Source: A Review of Critical Issues in High-Speed Vat Photopolymerization

**Paral, Lin, Cheng, Lin & Jeng, "A Review of Critical Issues in High-Speed Vat
Photopolymerization," *Polymers* 2023, 15(12):2716, doi:10.3390/polym15122716
(PMC10302688).**

## What it is

The master review aggregating separation-force physics and reduction methods —
the single richest secondary source behind KB-116/117/185.

## Key data

- Separation force (its Eq. 4, = Stefan): **`Pc = 3πμV/(2h³)·R⁴`** — verbatim
  *"inversely proportional to the third power of the dead zone thickness…directly
  proportional to the fourth power of the printed part's radius."*
- Force-reduction figures (by cited ref): S-PDMS ~13×; two-channel PDMS ~4–5% of
  baseline; hydrogel ~1/3 FEP; vibration ~60%; piezo ~75%; tilting ~20%; quartz
  (rigid) ~10× vs PDMS.
- Interface break "initiates from periphery to center" (crack propagation).
- CLIP dead zone ~20–30 µm; controlled by O₂ purity, photoinitiator, photon flux.
