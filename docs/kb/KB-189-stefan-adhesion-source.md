# Source: Stefan adhesion (squeeze-film viscous adhesion)

**Stefan adhesion — the viscous squeeze-film law (Stefan, 1874), standard
reference form.**

## What it is

The classical viscous-adhesion result underlying the suction regime of
bottom-up separation force (Pan's Eq. 6 is this law applied to a cured disk).

## Key data

- Force to separate two parallel discs through a viscous film:
  **`F = (3πηR⁴/2h³)·(dh/dt)`** (η = viscosity, R = radius, h = gap).
- With pull speed `V = dh/dt`: `F ∝ R⁴`, `F ∝ h⁻³`, `F ∝ V` — super-linear in
  area (R⁴ ∝ area²), extreme sensitivity to the gap.
- Physical basis: resin must flow radially inward to fill the widening gap;
  suction is worst at the centre and vents at the perimeter.
