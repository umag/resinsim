# Source: Formlabs — How to Hollow Out 3D Models

**Formlabs, "How to Hollow Out 3D Models" (blog).**

## What it is

Vendor hollowing guidance corroborating the cross-section-area → separation-force
link at the practitioner level.

## Key data

- Hollowing *"lowers the surface area of each layer, which decreases the force
  needed to detach the print…when moving to the next layer"* — reduces suction,
  warping, and support stress.
- Wall-thickness guidance consistent with Prusa (1 / 2 / 3 mm by part size);
  drain holes required to avoid trapped-vacuum cupping.

## Used by

KB-185 (hollowing lowers per-layer cured area → lower peel force).
