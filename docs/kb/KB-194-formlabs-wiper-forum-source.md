# Source: Formlabs forum — Why does the wiper/mixer blade run after every layer

**Formlabs Community forum thread, "Why does the wiper/mixer blade run after
every layer" (2018).**

## What it is

The only sourced explanation of the Formlabs wiper's purpose — important for
correctly *scoping* what is Formlabs' claim vs our interpretation.

## Key data

- There is **no Formlabs staff reply** in the thread. The sole explanation is a
  community user relaying Formlabs feedback: *"the wiper does do some mixing but
  is primarily designed to remove debris out of the printing path."*
- Stated purpose = **debris removal (primary) + some mixing**. **No oxygen, PDMS,
  re-oxygenation, or peel-force claim** appears anywhere in the thread.
- ⇒ any O₂/release-layer rationale for the wiper is *our mechanistic
  interpretation* (from CLIP/oxygen literature), not a Formlabs statement.
