# Liqcreate resin Dp/Ec at 405nm and 385nm

Measured by Liqcreate using Jacobs working curve method on DLP printer.

| Resin | Ec 405nm (mJ/cm²) | Dp 405nm (µm) | Ec 385nm (mJ/cm²) | Dp 385nm (µm) |
|-------|-------------------|---------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Premium Black | 5.00 | 170 | 2.47 | 90 |
| Premium White | 6.87 | 350 | 3.80 | 80 |
| Premium Tough | 14.47 | 560 | 7.22 | 120 |
| Clear Impact | 13.96 | 170 | 6.51 | 80 |
| Strong-X | 9.88 | 200 | 9.56 | 90 |
| Tough-X | 24.20 | 270 | 22.21 | 140 |
| Composite-X | 9.55 | 180 | 14.30 | 110 |

Key observations:
- Black resin has lowest Dp (170µm) due to pigment absorption
- Premium Tough has highest Dp (560µm) — clear/transparent formulation
- 385nm consistently has lower Dp than 405nm (higher energy photons, more absorption)
- Ec varies 5× across resins (5.0 to 24.2 mJ/cm²)
