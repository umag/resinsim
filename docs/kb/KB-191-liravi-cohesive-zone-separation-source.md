# Source: Cohesive-zone separation-force model (constrained-surface SL)

**Liravi, Das, Zhou, "Separation force analysis and prediction based on cohesive
element model for constrained-surface stereolithography," *Computer-Aided Design*
2015 (S0010448515000676).**

## What it is

The cohesive-zone / traction–separation FEA basis for modelling the peel crack —
the source of our critical energy-release-rate value.

## Key data

- Critical energy-release-rate **Gc ≈ 1.862×10⁻³ J/mm² (≈ 1862 J/m²)** for
  bilinear traction–separation damage evolution.
- Fracture "initiates from the periphery of the cured part and gradually spreads
  to the center" — confirms the peel-front-propagation picture.
- Cohesive law `k = σ_m/δ_n`, `γ = σ_m·δ_m/2`.
