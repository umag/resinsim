# Source: Prusa Knowledge Base — Hollowing

**Prusa Knowledge Base, "Hollowing."**

## What it is

Practical hollowing / drain-hole guidance — the source for our wall-thickness and
cupping-avoidance numbers.

## Key data

- Hollowing reduces per-layer cured cross-section → lower separation force,
  warping, and support stress.
- **Drain/vent holes are mandatory:** ≥2 holes, ≥3.5 mm diameter, ≥1 placed high
  — otherwise a sealed hollow acts as a suction cup ("cupping"/vacuum).
- Typical wall thickness: ~1 mm (small) / 2 mm (medium) / 3 mm (large) parts.
