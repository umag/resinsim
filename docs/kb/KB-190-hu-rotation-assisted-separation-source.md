# Source: Hu et al. — Rotation-Assisted Separation Model of Constrained-Surface SL

**Hu et al., "Rotation-Assisted Separation Model of Constrained-Surface
Stereolithography," *3D Printing and Additive Manufacturing* 2023, 10(1):146–155
(PMC10049864).**

## What it is

The dedicated rotation-separation study and a bilinear cohesive-zone model — the
source that disambiguates the rotation vs. tilting force-reduction figures.

## Key data

- **Rotation-assisted separation: up to 44% reduction in simulation**
  (1.065 → 0.596 N), **up to 14% measured** — distinct from the ~20% *tilting*
  result reported elsewhere.
- Uses a bilinear traction–separation cohesive-zone law; crack advances from edge
  to centre (rotation front-propagation lowers instantaneous peel-front load).
